About Me

I'm Reihaneh, a 23-year-old digital illustrator specialising in Procreate. Creating colored pencil paintings—and later, digital drawings—was my summer hobby from middle school until I became an undergraduate law student. It wasn’t until my gap year in 2024 that I began producing original digital artworks, which are inspired by technological challenges I read about and simple moments in life I appreciate. I'm still early in this stage, and as I find my way through art, I hope to continue my studies in IP and IT law and delve deeper into how law, creativity, and technology weave together.
